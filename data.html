PCA-LSTM Apple Futures Price Prediction - Data Source

Our data comes from several reliable sources to ensure accuracy and credibility for predictions.

  • Apple Futures Price Data: The historical Apple futures prices were sourced from the financial database, selecting the AP0 Apple continuous contract. web_index.
  • Volume Data: The volume of Apple futures contracts traded was sourced from web_index.
  • Temperature and Weather Data: Data related to weather conditions was collected from web_index.
  • Other influencing factors: such as international crude oil prices and the offshore RMB exchange rate, were downloaded from the Investing.com website web_index.

These datasets are pre-processed and used in conjunction to create a comprehensive model for price prediction.
